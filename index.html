Artificial Intelligence

Artifical intelligence or AI is a field of information technology which inables machines to mimic human behaviour.

Goals Of AI

The goal of AI is to create and develop computer systems and algorithms that can perform tasks which typically requires human intelligence.

This encomposes a range of objectives which includes :-

  • Mimic human intelligence
  • Automation and efficiency
  • Data Analysis and pattern recogination
  • Enhancing decision making
  • Personalisation
  • Solving complex problems
  • Natural language understanding
  • Perception and sensing
  • Creativity and innovation
  • Ethical and responsible AI
